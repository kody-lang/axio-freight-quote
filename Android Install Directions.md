# AXIO Freight Quote — Install on Android 📱

Add the freight calculator to your phone's home screen so it opens like a regular app (works offline, full screen, no browser bar).

**Website:** https://axio-freight-quote.netlify.app

---

## Fastest way — Scan the QR code

1. Open your phone **Camera** (or Google Lens).
2. Point it at the **AXIO QR code**.
3. Tap the link that pops up — it opens the calculator in Chrome.
4. Follow **Step 3 onward** below to add it to your home screen.

---

## Step-by-step (Chrome)

1. Open **Chrome** on your Android phone.
2. Go to **axio-freight-quote.netlify.app**
3. Tap the **⋮ three-dot menu** in the top-right corner.
4. Tap **Add to Home screen** (on some phones it says **Install app**).
5. Tap **Add** / **Install** to confirm.
6. The **AXIO** icon now appears on your home screen — tap it to open the app full screen. ✅

> **Samsung Internet browser?** Tap the **≡ menu** (bottom-right) → **Add page to** → **Home screen** → **Add**.

---

## Tips
- It works offline after the first open, so you can quote without signal.
- If you don't see "Add to Home screen," make sure you're using **Chrome** (not a different browser tab).
- To update to the newest version, just open the app while connected to Wi-Fi/data — it refreshes automatically.
